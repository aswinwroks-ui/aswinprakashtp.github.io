I'm a BIM Modeler and Structural Specialist with over eight years in the AEC industry. My journey began with structural drafting at ULCCS, where I evolved from producing detailed 2D drawings to leading organization-wide BIM implementation programs.

I've developed comprehensive Revit templates, coordinated multi-disciplinary models across architectural, structural, and MEP systems, and worked on international projects including major developments in the UAE.

Beyond the technical, I bring a graphic design sensibility to my work — an eye for visual clarity and presentation that makes complex structural information both accessible and elegant. I believe the best BIM models aren't just accurate; they communicate.
